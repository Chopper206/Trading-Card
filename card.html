Intimidating Cat

The cat who never purrs.

  • Doom Gaze: Peer deep into target player's eyes, petrifying them for 2 turns.
  • Fierce Hiss: Target player loses courage for 2 turns and cannot defend.
  • Dig Deep: Intimidating Cat sinks claws into target player instantly winning the game
  • Spray: Target player is disgusted for 4 turns and is unable to attack for 1 turn.

    • Intimidating Cat was born of a litter so cold. Not much is known about this creature due to its unfriendly demeanor.
